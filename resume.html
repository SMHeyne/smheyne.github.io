HundrED

At HundrED we research and share inspiring innovations in education. We provide visibility to innovations that are valuable, impactful & scalable to foster an inclusive movement across the world.
I am part of the growing HundrED Community as I took part in the review process for the HundrED Global Collection 2024. As a HundrED Academy Member, I work to identify and recommend education innovations that are changing the way our children learn. I also try or support trials of innovations in my area of Digital & Analog applied games. Using my area of expertise in education, I help HundrED to identify innovations that are impactful and scalable!

Bündnis Bunter Harz

The "Bündnis Bunter Harz" is a regional alliance in the Harz district for civil courage and against the radical right.
The goal is to stand up confidently and clearly as a civil society against right-wing extremist positions and actors. The term right-wing extremism is a generic term for attitudes that reject democracy and the equality of all people.
I set up their webpage and take care of problems accouring on it.
